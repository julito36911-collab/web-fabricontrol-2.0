Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install software on my computer? ▼
Yes and No. FabriControl is a hybrid system. You can install it on your local server (for data privacy) or use our Cloud version. The mobile app for operators works on any smartphone browser.
Can I try before buying? ▼
Absolutely! Every plan includes a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
Is my data safe? ▼
Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption (AES-256). If you choose the Local Installation, your data never leaves your premises.
Can I install FabriControl on my own server? ▼
Yes! The Enterprise plan includes on-premise installation. Your data stays 100% private on your infrastructure.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? ▼
You can cancel anytime without penalties. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. We also provide a data export in CSV format.
Do you offer support in English? ▼
Yes, our support team is bilingual (Spanish/English). Enterprise plans also get a dedicated account manager.
How does the AI Chat work? ▼
FabriControl integrates Google Gemini AI. It's trained on our documentation and can answer questions about features, workflows, and troubleshooting. Available in Professional and Enterprise plans.
What are Parametric Pieces? ▼
Parametric Pieces let you create templates with variables like {Width}x{Height}. The system auto-generates part codes (e.g., Door-100x200). It's unique to FabriControl and saves hours on repetitive products.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Professional later? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. All your data, clients, and inventory will be preserved automatically.
