🔐 2. Accessing FabriControl

Login

  1. Open your browser (Chrome recommended)
  2. Go to: fabricontrol-1.emergent.host/login
  3. Enter your email and password
  4. Click "Log In"
💡 Tip: Bookmark the login URL for quick access. You can also add it to your phone's home screen as a PWA.

Password Recovery

If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. You'll receive a reset link by email.

Multi-Device Access

FabriControl is cloud-based, so you can log in from multiple devices simultaneously. All data is synced in real time across all users.

🔒 5. Backups and Security

Automatic Backups

FabriControl performs automatic daily backups of all your data. These are stored securely in the cloud and are managed by the system — you don't need to do anything.

Data Security

  • ✅ Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • ✅ Daily automatic backups
  • ✅ Data retained for 90 days after account cancellation
  • ✅ No data stored on your local devices

Manual Export

You can export your data to Excel at any time from any module. This gives you a local copy of your important records.

💡 Best Practice: Export your key reports (inventory, quotes, orders) to Excel weekly as an additional backup for your records.

🤖 6. AI Chat with Gemini

What is it?

An intelligent assistant built into FabriControl that answers questions about the system, guides users through tasks, and provides support 24/7.

What Can It Do?

  • ✅ Answer questions about how to use FabriControl
  • ✅ Give step-by-step instructions for any task
  • ✅ Explain features and modules
  • ✅ Troubleshoot common issues
  • ✅ Respond in Spanish, English, or Hebrew automatically

How to Configure It (Inside the App)

  1. Go to: Menu → Settings → AI Chat
  2. Select provider: Gemini
  3. Enter your API Key (or use Emergent LLM Key)
  4. Choose model: Gemini 1.5 Flash (recommended)
  5. Save and test

❓ 8. Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install anything?
No. FabriControl is 100% cloud-based. Open your browser, go to the login URL, and you're in.
Does it work on Mac, Linux, or mobile?
Yes. Any device with a modern browser and internet access works — Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
All your data remains secure for 90 days. You can reactivate and recover everything. After 90 days it is permanently deleted.
Can multiple users work simultaneously?
Yes. All plans include unlimited users. Everyone connects to the same cloud account and data syncs in real time.
What's the difference between the $195 and $290 plans?
Only the payment method. Both plans have exactly the same features. $195/year = one annual payment (better price). $290/year = monthly installments (more flexible).
How long does setup take?
Fill form: 2 minutes. Receive access: less than 24 hours. Initial configuration: 2 hours. Full team training: 1 day.
